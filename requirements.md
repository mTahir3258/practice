Inward–Outward Management Android Application
Scope of the System
The system is developed to streamline business operations and communication among the Company, Suppliers, and Customers. It enables the company to efficiently manage customer requirements while coordinating with suppliers for material availability and timely delivery. The workflow ensures that all customer needs are fulfilled based on supplier updates, improving transparency, accuracy, and responsiveness throughout the supply chain.
Functional Flow
A. Company Module
- Manage master data of materials, lots, boxes, and rates.
 - Create Material Requests for suppliers.
 - Generate Advance Receipts and Challans based on supplier confirmation.
 - Capture total material and plastic quantities (in kg or units) for each item.
 - Auto-calculate total cost based on rates and quantities entered.
 - Generate Supplier Bills directly from challans and track all supplier transactions.
B. Supplier Module
- View and respond to material requests from the company.
 - Select materials from dropdown lists linked to the company’s master data.
 - Input quantity, weight, and plastic quantity per lot or box.
 - Submit Dispatch Intimation which automatically updates the company’s inward register.
 - View challan details and payment status from the company side.
C. Customer Module
- Generate Invoices for customers based on material issued from the company.
 - Automatically create Customer Billing linked to each invoice.
 - Include material and plastic quantity details, box counts, and rate-based calculations.
 - Maintain billing history and invoice records for each customer.


Workflow Summary
1. Company raises a new material request.
2. Supplier receives the request and sends an Intimation specifying quantity, weight, and delivery details.
3. Company reviews and confirms the intimation, auto-generating a challan.
4. Supplier dispatches materials and updates the status.
5. Company verifies inward material, creates billing for supplier, and generates customer invoices accordingly.

 This structured workflow ensures accountability, accuracy, and a complete digital record for every inward–outward transaction.
Key Features
 - Role-based access: Company, Supplier, Customer
 - Real-time inward–outward material tracking
 - Master data management (materials, lots, boxes, plastic rates)
 - Auto rate and cost calculation based on material & plastic entries
 - Challan and invoice generation with unique numbering
 - Billing automation for both supplier and customer sides
 - Real-time notifications and in-app alerts
 - Document upload for dispatch and delivery proofs
 - Search, filter, and export transaction history
 - Centralized dashboard with analytics and reports
Technology Stack
